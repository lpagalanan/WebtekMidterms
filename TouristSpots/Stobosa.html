Stobosa Artwork

Inspired by Brazil’s Favela Paintings, an urban mural project over the slum quarters of Rio De Janiero, Stobosa is a gargantuan mural of colorful sunflowers covering 18,000 square meters of 180 homes using 2,800 gallons of eco-friendly paint. The place is collectively known as Stobosa, an acronym for the sitios of Stonehill, Botiw-tiw and Sadjap in Barangay Balili of Benguet’s capital La Trinidad.
