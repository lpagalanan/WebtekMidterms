Camp John Hay

Camp John Hay used to be the rest and recreational facility for employees of the military and Department of Defence of the United States. The Bell House, a historic structure named after Major General Franklin Bell is situated at the camp's Historical Core. Besides the house is the Bell Amphitheatre Camp John Hay's history is featured through markers installed at the History Trail and Secret Garden. The Cemetery of Negativism nearby or the Lost Cemetery is a small area within Camp John Hay Manor Hotel and Forest Lodge are the two hotels operated by the management of Camp John Hay and are situated within the area. Camp John Hay is very pleasant to family or group picnics.
