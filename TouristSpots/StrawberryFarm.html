Strawberry Farm

Located just 30 minutes from Baguio City, Strawberry Farm "opens it doors" for strawberry picking, A unique experience for the whole family, you get to pick fresh strawberries -- right alongside the Ibaloi farmers -- to bring home or consume while on vacation in the Summer Capital of the Philippines.

FARE RATE : Php 11

JEEPNEY(s) :

  • Tomay
  • Camp Dangwa
  • Acop-Shilan
  • Alapang
  • Buyagan/ Windyhill (Php 16)

LOCATION OF TERMINALS :

  • Magsaysay Avenue
  • Shuntog Road
